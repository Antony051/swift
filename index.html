Capabilities

Everything SWAT+ needs.
Nothing extra.

SWIFT handles the tedious parts of SWAT+ model preparation — file formatting, statistics computation, and data gap-filling — so you can focus on modelling.

🌧️
Multi-station Support
Processes any number of weather stations from transposed multi-station CSVs. Station names become filenames automatically.
📊
Full Statistics Engine
Computes all SWAT+ weather generator statistics — wet/dry probabilities, precipitation skew, temperature means and SDs — by station and month.
🛰️
NASA POWER Integration
Automatically fetches missing solar radiation, wind speed, or humidity from NASA POWER with parallel requests and in-session caching.
🏔️
Auto Elevation Fill
Missing station elevations are retrieved from OpenTopoData (SRTM 30m / ASTER 30m) — no manual DEM lookup needed.
🌿
Two ET Methods
Hargreaves-Samani (temperature only) or FAO-56 Penman-Monteith (full data). Choose what your data supports.
⚡
Works Offline
All core processing is local. API calls are optional — SWIFT gracefully estimates solar radiation via FAO-56 when offline.

Workflow

Three steps to
SWAT+ ready files.

01
Upload your data
Provide a stations metadata CSV and transposed multi-station CSVs for precipitation, Tmax, and Tmin. Solar, humidity, and wind are optional.
02
Configure settings
Set your simulation date range, choose an ET method, select your output folder. SWIFT resolves missing variables via NASA POWER or FAO-56.
03
Generate & use
Click Generate. All .pcp, .tmp, .slr, .hmd, .wnd, .pet files and monthly.csv are written directly to your SWAT+ project folder.

Output Files

All files SWAT+ expects.
Ready to load.

The complete set of SWAT+ weather inputs, formatted to specification and written directly to your project folder.

.pcp
Precipitation
Daily rainfall per station with header metadata
.tmp
Temperature
Daily Tmax and Tmin per station
.slr
Solar Radiation
From CSV, NASA POWER, or FAO-56 estimate
.hmd
Humidity
Daily dewpoint — from RH%, dewpoint CSV, or NASA POWER
.wnd
Wind Speed
Daily wind in m/s — from CSV or NASA POWER
.pet
Potential ET
Daily ET₀ via Hargreaves-Samani or Penman-Monteith
.cli
Climate Index Files
Auto-generated index files for each variable type
monthly.csv
Weather Generator Stats
Complete monthly statistics table for SWAT+ wgn
stations.csv
Updated Station Metadata
Elevation auto-filled + number of rain years computed
